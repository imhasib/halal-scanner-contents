1. Who We Are

Speaking Club ("we", "us", or "our") is a language learning platform that connects users for real-time voice and video conversation practice. Our service is available as a mobile application (Android & iOS) and as a web application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information about you when you use Speaking Club. By using our service you agree to the practices described here.

If you have questions about this policy or your personal data, email us at email2hasib@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

We only collect data that is necessary to operate and improve the service.

Category Examples Required?
Account data Name, email address, password (hashed), preferred language, proficiency level Required
Profile data Profile photo, bio, country, learning goals Optional
Usage data Session timestamps, call duration, features used, matchmaking queue activity Required
Communication data WebRTC audio/video streams during active calls (peer-to-peer, not stored on our servers) Required
AI Practice data Text or audio submitted to AI practice sessions for feedback generation Optional
Device & technical data IP address, device type, OS version, app version, crash reports Required

Microphone & Camera: We request microphone and camera permissions solely to enable voice and video calls. We do not record, store, or transmit your audio or video beyond the live peer-to-peer call session.

Cookies & Local Storage: The web application uses browser local storage to persist your authentication token and preferences. We use no third-party advertising cookies.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • Provide the service — create your account, authenticate you, match you with conversation partners, and facilitate calls.
  • Personalization — show relevant partners based on your language level and learning goals.
  • AI Practice — generate feedback on your spoken or written English using AI; input is processed and then discarded.
  • Safety & abuse prevention — detect and act on reports of inappropriate conduct.
  • Service improvement — analyse aggregated, anonymised usage patterns to make the product better.
  • Communications — send transactional emails (password reset, important service notices). We do not send marketing emails without your explicit opt-in.
We do not sell your personal data, rent it, or use it for targeted advertising.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share your data only in limited circumstances:

  • Other users — your display name, profile photo, and language level are visible to matched conversation partners. Your email and private details are never shared with other users.
  • Infrastructure providers — we use cloud hosting, database, and Redis services. These providers process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements and do not use your data for their own purposes.
  • AI service provider — AI Practice content is sent to an AI provider API for processing. Input is not retained by the provider beyond the request.
  • Legal obligations — we may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

We require all third-party processors to maintain appropriate security standards and to process data only as instructed by us.

5. Data Retention

Data type Retention period
Account & profile data Until you delete your account, then 30 days before permanent deletion
Call history & session logs 90 days
AI practice transcripts Not retained — discarded after feedback is generated
Crash & error logs 30 days
Anonymised analytics Up to 2 years

When you delete your account, all personally identifiable data is deleted within 30 days except where we are required by law to retain it longer.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+.
  • Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes — we never store plain-text passwords.
  • Authentication uses short-lived JWT tokens; no session data is stored server-side.
  • Real-time calls use WebRTC peer-to-peer encryption (DTLS-SRTP) — audio and video travel directly between devices and are never routed through our servers.
  • Access to production databases is restricted to authorised personnel only.

No system is perfectly secure. If you suspect a security issue, please contact us at email2hasib@gmail.com immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at email2hasib@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. Account deletion is also available directly in the app under Settings → Delete Account.

8. Children's Privacy

Speaking Club is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at email2hasib@gmail.com and we will delete the account promptly.

Users aged 13–17 must have parental or guardian consent before using the service where required by local law.
